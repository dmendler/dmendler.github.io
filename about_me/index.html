About Me

I am a Junior pursuing a Computer Science degree within the College of Engineering. I am also working towards a minor in Engineering Corporate Practice. Outside of the classroom, I am member of the Junior Class Council and a member of the Notre Dame Rugby team. I also co-founded the Robinson Center Chess Instruction Club, where we help young kids at the Robinson Learning Center learn how to play the game of chess. I am also an Eagle Scout, participating in the boy scout program for seven years. I am originally from Wayne, Pennsylvania, which is a suburb of Philadelphia. Before Notre Dame, I attended Malvern Preparatory School in Malvern, Pennsylvania.
